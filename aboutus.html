WHO WE ARE


The Gold Life Merchandise Manufacturing Company Limited is an enterprise registered in Hong Kong; however, it caters customers from all over the world. The company co-operates with different Chinese manufacturer for those commodities which could not be self manufactured.

Mr. Nimesh Pyakurel and Mr. Bhishma Raj Aryal are managing the company. We (Mr. Aryal and Pyakurel) are the old hands in the terms of understanding, manufacturing and supplying toys and artifacts. We entered in this field in 2009 and 2012 respectively driven by the passion of opening up one of the dominant companies in China.

We worked with European Trading Companies for many years and gained practical experience in toy designing, manufacturing and trading. Finally, we decided to open up our own enterprise. Considering all facts, today, our company is having an edge over the others.
